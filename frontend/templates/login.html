ARA is a software tool that guides students through some portion of the SQ3R active reading technique. SQ3R stands for Survey, Question, Read, Recite, Review. SQ3R provides students (and other readers) with a structured approach for reading textbooks and other technical material. One basic idea of SQ3R is that learning technical material is best accomplished by first gaining an understanding the high-level structure of a chapter (or other technical text), and then pursuing a more-detailed understanding of the material, such as by generating questions, and reading the material primarily to answer those questions (McKeachie & Svinicki, 2013).
